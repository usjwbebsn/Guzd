Who We Are

ABOUT
gifonte

gifonte is a free, open suite of Unicode text tools built for creators, designers, and anyone who cares about how their words look. It started as a single font generator and is growing into a full creative text platform.

Every tool is built as a static web app — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only. No servers. No databases. No accounts. No subscriptions. Just useful tools that work instantly in any modern browser, on any device, anywhere in the world.

The project is maintained independently. Follow on TikTok @neravdz for new tool announcements, updates, and creative text inspiration.

Mission

Typography is one of the most underused tools in everyday digital communication. gifonte exists to make typographic creativity accessible to everyone — not just graphic designers — through simple, fast, free tools.

Current Tools

Font Generator — Transform text into 30 unique Unicode styles across 9 categories: Mathematical, Calligraphy, Gothic, Sans-Serif, Symbols, Stylized, Aesthetic, Diacritic, and Glitch.

Upcoming Tools

Text Analyzer · Lorem Generator · CSS Text Effects · Font Pairing Tool · Character Counter. All tools will be free, client-side, and privacy-respecting.

Contact & Community

All communication happens through TikTok: @neravdz. Feature requests, bug reports, and collaboration proposals are all welcome.

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They are widely used to remember preferences and improve user experience.

Our Use of Cookies

gifonte uses no tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The only browser storage used is localStorage (not a cookie) to remember your light/dark mode preference. This is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Cookies

Google Fonts may set cookies or use browser caching to optimize font delivery. This is governed by Google's Cookie Policy. gifonte has no control over these cookies and does not access them.

Managing Storage

You can clear all gifonte local storage at any time via your browser settings under "Clear Site Data" or "Clear Browsing Data". This will reset your theme preference to the default.
