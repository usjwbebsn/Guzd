PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

gifonte ("we", "our", "the platform") is a collection of free, client-side web tools for text style generation and typography. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data, which can be summarized simply: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

— The text you type into any tool.
— Your name, email address, or any identifying information.
— Usage data, heatmaps, session recordings, or behavioral analytics.
— IP addresses, geolocation, or device fingerprints.
— Cookies beyond essential theme preference storage (local only).
— Crash reports or performance telemetry.

3. Local Storage

gifonte stores one value in your browser's localStorage: your selected display theme (light or dark mode). This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's local site data. No other data is written to localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB.

4. Third-Party Services

gifonte uses Google Fonts to load typography. When your browser loads a page, it may make a request to Google's servers to fetch font files. Google's own Privacy Policy governs this interaction. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising networks.

gifonte does not include any tracking scripts, social media embeds, or third-party widgets that could fingerprint or identify you.

5. How Our Tools Work

All text transformations on gifonte run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your input text is processed locally and never transmitted to any server. There is no backend infrastructure receiving or storing your content.

6. Children's Privacy

gifonte is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no data from anyone, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if we add features that change how data is handled. Any meaningful changes will be announced on our TikTok account @neravdz and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, reach out via TikTok: @neravdz.
