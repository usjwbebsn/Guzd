Why gifonte

FEATURES

01 — Client-Side
Runs In Your Browser
Every operation — font generation, video processing, GIF encoding — happens locally via WebAssembly and JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.
02 — Universal
Works Everywhere
Generated Unicode text pastes directly into Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, Discord, WhatsApp. GIF output is a standard file compatible with every platform.
03 — No Compromise
Quality First
The GIF converter uses a dithering-optimized encoder with configurable palette and frame rate. Results are sharp, not washed out like most online converters.
04 — Private
Zero Tracking
No analytics, no advertising pixels, no session recording. The only storage used is a single theme preference key — it never leaves your device.
About gifonte

THE
VISION

gifonte.com is a collection of browser-based creative tools for people who care about the details of how their content looks and moves.

The name combines GIF and fonte — the Italian and Portuguese word for font. Two mediums, one platform. Started with a text style generator, now expanding into media tools.

Everything is built as static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no backend, no dependencies, runs everywhere. Follow the project on TikTok @neravdz.
