About This Tool

ABOUT THE
CONVERTER

→ Engine
FFmpeg.wasm
gifonte uses FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly — the same C-based engine behind professional video tools like Handbrake and DaVinci Resolve. Running at near-native speed in your browser, it's orders of magnitude faster than JavaScript canvas-based encoders.
→ Quality
2-Pass Palette
GIF supports 256 colors per frame. gifonte uses FFmpeg's 2-pass strategy: Pass 1 analyzes the video to build an optimal palette. Pass 2 maps every frame to that palette with Lanczos scaling and your chosen dithering algorithm. The result is cinema-quality GIFs.
→ Performance
Optimization Tips
For fastest results: use 320–480px width, 10–12 fps, duration under 5s, quality 15+. For highest quality: use 640px+, 15–20 fps, quality 1–5, Floyd-Steinberg dithering. FFmpeg loads once (~5 MB) and is cached — subsequent conversions start instantly.
→ Privacy
100% Local
Your video never leaves your device. FFmpeg.wasm runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. No uploads, no server processing, no account required. Files are read into browser memory and discarded when you close the tab.
